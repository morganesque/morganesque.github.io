This was great fun if a little mind melting at first. Dean Vipond had been asked to design an animation to be displayed on large screens at conferences to advertise the company. Dean’s concept consisted of a never ending zoom into various grids, each revealing different messages and images conveying the idea of the multiple levels at which the company worked. I produced the animation in Flash after a bit of head scratching over how to achieve it followed by a “Eureka!” moment where everything finally clicked into place.
